# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-06

## 1. Controller / Contact
Robert Freese  
Email: robert.freese@gmx.de

## 2. Summary
We take your privacy seriously. This app is designed to work **without an account** and to process your content **locally on your device**.

- **No ads**
- **No tracking**
- **No analytics / crash reporting services**
- **No server-side storage by us**

## 3. What the app does (media to notifications)
The app lets you create local notifications that can include:
- a photo you take with the camera, or
- an image/video you select from your device gallery, or
- media you share to the app via Android’s **Share** feature (Share Intent).

All of this is handled **on-device**. We do **not** upload your media to our servers.

## 4. Permissions

### Camera (`android.permission.CAMERA`)
The app requests the **Camera** permission only to let you take a photo (or record a video) that you can then attach to a local notification.

- Camera input is processed **only on your device**.
- We do **not** transmit camera content to us or any third party.
- Any saving of photos/videos happens via your device storage (e.g., the system camera / media storage) and remains on your device unless **you** choose to share it elsewhere.

### Photos / Media (Gallery access)
The app can access media you select from your device gallery. The app only reads the media you explicitly choose, and uses it locally to render the notification.

## 5. Data collection, storage, and sharing

### Data we collect
We do **not** collect personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking data.

### Data we store
The app may store **local app data** on your device (e.g., settings, drafts, or locally created content) to provide the functionality. This data remains on your device.

### Data we share
We do **not** sell or share personal data with third parties.

## 6. Sharing content (Share Intent)
If you use Android’s Share feature to share an image/video to this app, the shared content is used **only** to create a local notification on your device. The app does not upload that content.

## 7. Your choices
- You can deny or revoke the camera permission at any time in Android system settings. Some features may not work without it.
- You can choose which media to select and share; the app only uses what you explicitly provide.

## 8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at this URL.
