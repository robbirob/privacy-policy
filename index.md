# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-05

## 2. What data we process
Our app requests the Android permission **`android.permission.CAMERA`** to enable the following feature:

- **Take fotos to send to yourself as a notification.**

## 3. Camera usage
- The camera stream is processed **only on the device**.
- We do **not** upload camera images/videos to our own servers.
- We do **not** store camera images/videos unless you **explicitly** save them via the app’s user interface.

## 4. Data sharing
- We do **not** sell personal data.
- We do **not** share camera data with third parties.

## 5. Third-party services
The app may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide, personalize, and measure ads.  
Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Your choices
You can deny or revoke the camera permission at any time in Android system settings. Some features may not work without it.

## 7. Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version is always available at this URL.
